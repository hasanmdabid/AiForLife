Hello,
I have received my bachelor degree in Electrical and Electonics Engineering from American International-University, Dhaka, Bnagladesh, in 2013, and Master of Science in Mechatronics Engineering from University of Siegen, NRW, Germany. I joined City University, BD in August 2017 as an Lecturer Under Deparment of EEE . Now I am working as a Senior Lecturer at American International University-Bangladesh under Dept. of CoE. At different academic level, I was awarded both national and international awards and funds (i.e Erasmus Mundus Scholarship, EU Project-My Active Healthy Aging, BD goverment scholarship etc) for my performance & research. I have been involved in several IEEE conferences in various capacities. I have served as a reviewer for Computer System Networking and Telecommunications and IEEE conferences.
My research domain includes but not limited to Stochastic signal analysis, Machine learning, Pattern recognition, State Estimation, Digital control, Navigation. If you are interested to join my team,feel free to write me an email.
